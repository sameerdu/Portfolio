BRIEFCASE


For the bag design project, the task is to choose one brand and focus on one bag. The project requires conducting market research on the brand and its competitors. The aim is to gain insights into the bag's design, features, target audience, and making one bag for the brand.

For this project I have chosen 'Hidesign' as a brand to focus on, the type of bag is Briefcase.

The leather briefcase bag has a unique design that allows it to open from both sides. It features a large main compartment in the back, perfect for bulky items. The front side has three compartments, one for a laptop and two for files or documents, ensuring easy organization. There's a small compartment for keeping small items like phone, wallet, pens, keys, etc. The back pocket is ideal for a water bottle and additional books or clothes. Its stylish design and multiple compartments make it a practical accessory for busy professionals and students. The leather material ensures durability, making it in need of a long-lasting and practical bag.
