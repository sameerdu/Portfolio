ROOTED DREAMS

Wearable art has gained popularity in recent years, with a growing trend towards incorporating unconventional materials and avant-garde designs that challenge traditional notions of fashion and art.

This project is a creative exploration of wearable art, combining the symbolism of the root chakra with surrealistic design elements to create a unique and visually striking headgear.

The headgear that represents the Root chakra is a large and durable piece worn on the head. Its unique design resembles the deep roots of a tree, symbolizing grounding and stability. The headgear is held in place with the help of helmet and it is designed in way that it will balance the weight of headgear so that it doesn't require straps or something to tie and covers a significant portion of the head. It serves as a reminder to stay rooted in the present moment and to overcome life's challenges. The headgear is a powerful symbol of inner strength and resilience and can help individuals connect with their personal values and goals.
